EN-US:
Create a company time calculator:

Hire date: 01/01/2020
Current date: use LocalDate.now()
Use Period.between() to calculate years, months, and days
Use ChronoUnit.DAYS.between() to calculate total days worked
Show: "5 years, 1 month and 11 days (1868 total days)"

Comment: Period gives you the "human" difference (years/months/days), while ChronoUnit gives you the absolute total. Both are useful depending on context!

PT-BR:
Crie um calculador de tempo de empresa:

Data de admissão: 01/01/2020
Data atual: use LocalDate.now()
Use Period.between() para calcular anos, meses e dias
Use ChronoUnit.DAYS.between() para calcular o total de dias trabalhados
Mostre: "5 anos, 1 mês e 11 dias (1868 dias totais)"
